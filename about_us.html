Our Story

Thrifting is the quality of using money and other resources carefully and not wastefully. ThriftyCulture was founded by Shandukani Radzilani, someone with a unique taste in fashion, during his final year of high school in 2023. He realized a demand for the type of clothing he wore – most of which were vintage items.

The initial business model involved selling these items through the ThriftyCulture Instagram page. ThriftyCulture is for people with a timeless style looking for value for money. It’s a place to find affordable clothing you can’t find anywhere else.

Welcome to ThriftCulture, where every item has a story, and every purchase makes a difference. Established in 2024, this is more than a store; it’s a community hub that promotes sustainability and offers affordable, high-quality clothing.
